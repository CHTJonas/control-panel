New Society

Please enter the full name of your society; e.g. "CU Gaelic Athletic Club". Abreviation of "Cambridge University" to "CU" is encouraged.

This should be a suitable society or short name for your society, 16 or fewer lowercase letters only - e.g. "cugac" for the Cambridge University Gaelic Athletic Club


Please enter the CRSIDs of all the society admins. A person's CRSID is the string before the "@cam.ac.uk" in his or her University email address. Please include yourself in this list. It is strongly recommended to nominate at least one additional administrator besides yourself, in order to maintain continuity of the account in the event of an administrator leaving the University. Accounts with no active admins may be suspended without warning.


Please enter the list names for any mailing lists that you require. Mailing lists will have addresses of the form -@srcf.net. should contain only alphanumeric characters and hyphens. Do not include society name or @srcf.net.

Databases will have the same name as the society account. You can have both MySQL and PostgreSQL databases if you so desire.
