To apply for a shared society account on the SRCF, please fill in the form below. Once the society account is created, you will be able to use the control panel to create any databases or mailing lists.

Society accounts are identified by a short name, which will form part of your society website URL (name.soc.srcf.net) and mailing addresses (e.g. name-admins@srcf.net). Short names cannot be changed after account creation, so choose wisely.

Please note that all named users that wish to be administrators on the account must have their own personal SRCF accounts – if this is not the case, direct them to the signup form to apply. It is strongly recommended to nominate at least one additional administrator besides yourself, in order to maintain continuity of the account in the event of an administrator leaving the University. Accounts with no active admins may be suspended without warning.
